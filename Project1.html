Website Critiques

Questions:

  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information?(Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)
  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?
  8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes(responsive design)?
  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

Pitchfork

www.pitchfork.com

  1. My first impression of this site was that I thought it was a little boring and bland. It isn't very eye pleasing.
  2. I didn't care for the flow and wasn't impressed with the organization of it. They didn't have links specifically on the left or right, they were throughout the site.
  3. It wasn't structured great but it still wasn't confusing to navigate.
  4. Graphics are dull in my opinion and not very eye catching. I felt it wasn't a great layout and kind of bland because of the mostly black and white imaging.
  5. The home page loaded fairly quickly and reasonably.
  6. The text color and size were reasonable. It used different sizes and fonts.
  7. The navigation bar is at the top of the page and easily visible. It was well done because it is in a logically location on the page.
  8. The first page was mostly black and white in color and design while the next pages had more colorful images and setup.
  9. Smaller window size didn't change its attributes and it retained its set up.
  10. Article layout is easy to follow and navigate. I felt it was a little unusual to be so bland in design.
